About the app

Wellducation helps motivate college students to prioritize their health. We know that students struggle to balance schoolwork with eating right, exercising, and getting a good night's sleep. They know their health is important, but they don't have the motivation to put self-care first. That's where Wellducation comes in. By using instant gratification, our app motivates college students to take care of themselves and have fun meeting their health goals.

How It Works

Wellducation is an app that helps students prioritize their health by motivating them through competition and rewards.

Choose your goals

Pick between three catgories that you want to work on for the week: diet, sleep, or exercise. Then choose how often you want to complete them. The bigger the goal, the more potential points you can earn!

Earn points and redeem them for rewards

Every time you meet a goal, you will earn a set amount of points. Those points can be redeemed for rewards, like coupons at healthy restaurants and stores.

Connect with friends and engage in competition

Add friends in the app and see how your points stack up compared to them. Wellducation will display the top points earner among your friends.
